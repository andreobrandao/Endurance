Books i like

The little prince

The little prince is a book telling the story of a wonderful boy that lives in a tiny asteroid
alone with his little vulcans and with the non-endless job of cutting off the baobas, trees that
if not cuted, would eventually grow big and take all the space of his little home.
Coming from somewhere in the space a seed finds his little world and there decide to grow, he takes
care of that plant, that after waking up, she reveals herself as being a rose, and what a beaultiful
rose... The prince has as his priority to attend to the roses's wishes, even though of her vanity.
But someday he decides to leave his asteroid to learn. After the fare thee well wishes he leaves and
passes through many worlds and meet many creatures, untill the day he arrives on earth and meet the
aviator, friend that will find in the little boy the mysteries and beauty of an enlightened spirit,
along with the teaching the little boy itself learned in his journey, teachings of friendship,
commitment, human desires, solitude and the most important, how to love.

The prophet

This book follows the history of a man who passed some long time in a city called orphalesea
as a common man along with the other citizens, but with the arrival of a ship from his natal
city he realize his time is done at that place, and he decides to walk from the upper hill
to the harbour where he would take the ship, but while he walks with his things in hand, people
from orphalesea realizes that much knowledge is what they could have taken from him, and one last
time they surround him as he walks asking him to speak about important things in life, about Love,
about work, about marriage, about God, about our kids, about beauty and much more. The prophet
brings wise words, and with his thoughts a lot for us to think and find a deeper meaning to our lifes
It's a short book, but if you let yourself remain in his words you can find that the whole book holds
more than it may appear at a first sight, it's for sure one of my favorite books of all time and
i hope you can feel the beauty from this amazing work written by khalil Gibran.
